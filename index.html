About

Hi, I'm Floophy

I'm a digital and 3D artist working mostly within the vore theme, always kept SFW.

I try to bring something genuinely original to every piece, and I aim to make the most wholesome work I can. My goal is simple: put a smile on the face of anyone who stops by, while making something that impresses at the same time.

Everything is built in Blender, and I like to keep things varied, mixing memes with more serious pieces, so there's something for everyone to enjoy.
