Terms of Service

Please read the following terms carefully before placing a commission. Ordering means you accept them.

General

I work as a digital and 3D artist creating custom illustrations and character designs. All pieces are delivered digitally as high-resolution PNG or JPG files.

Ordering & communication

Commissions can be requested via Discord, Telegram, or email.

A detailed description and reference images are needed where possible, unless you're requesting a character design, in which case no reference is required.

Work begins only once every detail has been confirmed and payment, or the deposit, has been received.

Payment

Payment methods: bank transfer or Revolut.

Payment can be made in full upfront, or as a 50% deposit.

Final files are only delivered once the full amount has been received.

Payments already made are generally non-refundable, unless the work cannot be completed on my end.

Turnaround time

Turnaround depends on the complexity of the project and the current queue.

It typically ranges from a few days to a few weeks.

If there's a significant delay, I'll let you know.

Revisions

Minor revisions during the sketch stage are free.

Larger changes, or changes requested after approval, may carry an extra fee.

What I won't draw

NSFW or sexual content

Extreme or offensive content

Illegal or hateful subject matter

Direct copies of other artists' work

Usage rights

Clients are granted personal use of the finished artwork.

I retain the copyright to all completed pieces.

I may showcase finished work in my portfolio, on social media, or on my website, unless otherwise agreed.

Commercial use is only permitted with prior agreement.

Cancellations & refunds

You may cancel an order before the sketch stage begins.

Once work has started, a partial or full refund is not guaranteed.

If I'm unable to complete the work, you'll receive a proportional refund.

Accepting these terms

Placing a commission means you confirm you've read, understood, and accepted the Terms of Service above.

Thank you for choosing me. I always aim to bring your ideas to life at the best quality I can.
